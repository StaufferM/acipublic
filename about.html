About ACI

Your Local, Family Owned General Contractor

Our Story

ACI Construction has been around since 1993. Our owner, Tim Stauffer, is an electrical engineer by degree who started out at Battelle Memorial Institute. From there, he started his own company, Advanced Concepts, Inc. Our origins started in Canal Winchester, our first office has been right outside of Canal for the past 20 years. Along the journey, we have opened offices in Lima, Delaware, Upper Arlington, and Milford. We service all of Ohio as well as the Tri State area. We now have multiple degreed engineers on our team and have project managers that are solely dedicated to residential homeowners and project managers that focus on commercial clients. Our biggest strength is our ability to handle and coordinate complex jobs.
