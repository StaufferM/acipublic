Peace of Mind

As a family-owned and locally operated business, we take immense pride in serving our community with top-tier roofing solutions. Our commitment to excellence is rooted in decades of experience, ensuring that every project meets the highest industry standards. We understand the unique challenges that local homeowners face, from unpredictable weather to long-term durability, and we tailor our services to provide lasting protection. Our team prioritizes transparency, reliability, and customer satisfaction, treating every home as if it were our own. When you choose us, you're not just getting a new roof—you’re getting a trusted partner dedicated to safeguarding your home for years to come.

Don't get pressured into a new roof when all you want is a repair.

Our Project Managers know that when a homeowner wants a repair - that is what they will offer. We do not use high pressure sales tactics, we're here to get your home back to being protected. We will provide our recommendations and evidence that backs our recommendation, but it is only to educate homeowners.

❌ Water Damage - It doesn't take long for a leak to lead to water damage. As soon as evidence of a leak is identified you need a professional to diagnose and stop the leak before it leads to costly repairs.
❌ Insurability - Insurance carriers are becoming more stringent on roof insurability, keep your home insured with a watertight roof.
❌ High Cost - With our extensive knowledge and experience we can generally offer multiple options for repair to fit any homeowners budget.
❌ NO HIGH PRESSURE SALES - No one wants to deal with having to say "no" five times - with ACI, you won't have to say "no" once.

Our team will provide a roof report and recommendations no matter what service you plan on selecting - we believe it is our responsiblity to inform and educate the homeowner of their current situation and potential issues down the road.
