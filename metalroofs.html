Metal Roofs

The Best Metal Roofing Contractor in Ohio

Metal Roofing requires extensive knowledge

The most common responses from homeowners we've received is that when they ask any roofing company they can find online if they do metal roofs, the answer is always yes. Metal roofing requires lots of experience and knowledge with metal roofing systems. Most roofers cannot explain the different options between gauges, failure points, correct installation, and manufacturer requirements.

Knowledge is everything

At ACI, we have dedicated Metal Trade Experts to educate you of your options and the positives and negatives of each. With the countless options Metal Roofs have, you need a contractor who is knowledgeable in every aspect. We can custom quote any spec you would like - whether you're chasing after just that metal look or if you want a true maintenance free roof.

✅ Standing Seam - The top of the line option. Reach out to us to find out why our standing seam option can be customized to be completely maintenance free.
✅ Performance Panel - When you want the metal roof look without the metal roof price.
✅ Strong Warrantys - Feel at ease knowing your metal roof will be backed by the manufacturer.
✅ Education - We don't just hand out quotes - you'll get a conversation informing you of all the options and possibilities of a metal roof.

A correctly installed metal roof is one of the most resilient roofing systems out there. Don't trust a big purchase and the protection of your home with just any roofing contractor - get informed (by us) first, and see for yourself how our knowledge and experience sets us apart.
