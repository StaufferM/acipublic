Media Blasting

The best surface prep available

Media Blasting


✅ Walnut Blasting - Walnut media blasting is a gentle yet effective method for removing paint, carbon buildup, and coatings without damaging delicate surfaces, making it ideal for automotive, aerospace, and restoration projects. It is also biodegradable and non-toxic, providing an eco-friendly alternative to harsh chemical stripping and abrasive blasting methods.
✅ Cob Blasting - Cob blasting is an eco-friendly surface cleaning method that uses biodegradable corn cob granules to remove paint, dirt, and debris without damaging the underlying material. It is a gentle yet effective solution for delicate surfaces such as wood, log homes, and historical restorations, providing a non-abrasive alternative to traditional blasting techniques.

Cob blasting is an ideal solution for restoring delicate surfaces without causing damage. It is commonly used for log home restoration, effectively removing old finishes, mold, and grime while preserving the wood's integrity. This method is also excellent for historical building preservation, as it gently cleans stone and brickwork without eroding the structure. Additionally, cob blasting is a preferred choice for industrial equipment maintenance, as it removes coatings and buildup without harming sensitive components.
